Project Description

Studio Plus is the first kitchen specialist in Croatia, and their goal is to offer you everything you need to furnish your kitchen in one place. They are a proud partner of Nobilia and Hacker kitchens, the largest German kitchen manufacturers in Europe. They also offer the largest range of white goods on the market from manufacturers Gorenje , Bosch and Miele . All these brands are synonymous with quality, reliability and a long tradition. The combination of German quality and their expertise will allow you to become the owner of your dream kitchen. For this client, we created a Kitchen Designer - a custom-programmed solution in which customers can sign up for free design in Studio Plus salons.

How can I set up the kitchen system?

A kitchen is no longer just a place to cook; it’s the heart of your home, where you gather with family and friends. Whether you're preparing a quick meal or hosting a dinner party, having the right kitchen system makes all the difference. The perfect kitchen is one that blends functionality with style, and with Studio Plus, you can achieve just that.

  • Choose quality materials: Opt for high-end finishes that will stand the test of time.
  • Ensure proper layout: Maximize space with an efficient kitchen layout that works for your needs.
  • Include modern appliances: Equip your kitchen with reliable, energy-efficient white goods from trusted brands.
  • Personalize with style: Customize your kitchen system with features that reflect your personal taste and lifestyle.

Studio Plus - Your Kitchen Specialist

Studio Plus is the first kitchen specialist in Croatia, offering everything you need to furnish your kitchen under one roof. As proud partners of Nobilia and Hacker kitchens, Europe’s leading German kitchen manufacturers, we provide top-tier products with unmatched quality.

We also offer the largest selection of white goods from renowned brands like Gorenje, Bosch, and Miele—brands that are synonymous with quality, reliability, and long tradition. With a perfect combination of German craftsmanship and our expertise, we help you create the kitchen of your dreams.

For this client, we developed the Kitchen Designer—a custom-programmed solution allowing customers to sign up for a free design consultation at Studio Plus salons, making the process of designing your dream kitchen easier than ever.
