Luceed ERP: Your Complete Domestic Business Solution

Our industry-tailored Luceed ERP system integrates all business processes - from procurement to accounting - in real-time across Croatia and the region. With 20+ years of practical experience, we've optimized it for retail, wholesale, production, and service businesses.

Why Luceed ERP Stands Out

  • Real-time data: Monitor inventory, sales, and finances across all locations
  • Multi-language support: Croatian, English, Slovenian, Serbian
  • Seamless integrations: Webshops, production lines, courier services, and more
  • Custom reporting: Generate OLAP reports from a single database

Key Modules & Capabilities

Retail Solutions

  • Real-time POS tracking across all locations and shifts
  • Automated branch replenishment and stock monitoring
  • Integrated loyalty programs and promotional systems
  • Full fiscal compliance for Balkan region countries

Wholesale Management

  • End-to-end order tracking from request to delivery
  • Multi-level partner pricing and rebate controls
  • Procurement planning based on sales forecasts
  • eInvoicing and traffic analysis tools

Warehouse (WMS) & Logistics

  • Optimized picking routes and positional warehousing
  • LOT/SKU tracking with FEFO expiration management
  • Courier service integrations with package tracking
  • Real-time warehouse dashboards

Production & Service

  • Production order monitoring with cost calculation
  • Service order lifecycle management
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling
  • Equipment history tracking

Accounting & HR

  • Automated bank statement processing
  • Multi-currency and interest calculations
  • Payroll for all worker types (regular, contractors, etc.)
  • Continuous legal compliance updates

Implementation Process

We guide you through every step with department-specific training to ensure maximum ROI. Our specialists analyze your workflows to customize Luceed for your exact needs.
