Journey was started

e-Sustavi doo, founded in 2008, is a Croatian IT company specializing in modern software solutions for businesses.

Our services consist of preparing companies for the digitalization of their business processes by introducing Luceed ERP or Odoo ERP/CRM software solutions, website development, webshop development integrated with Luceed/Odoo ERP systems, hosting solutions, and business optimization in more mature business phases.

Team Expansion and Culture

Our team includes programmers, designers, project managers, analysts, support, and sales professionals.

e-Sustavi doo is a team of about 10 employees with different profiles, knowledge and competencies who have so far digitized more than 300 companies in Croatia with Luceed and Odoo software solutions delivered at the highest level of quality.
